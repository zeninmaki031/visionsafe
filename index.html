Reading Guide Overlay
Move your mouse over the text below. The green guide helps track your reading position — a useful tool for dyslexia and low vision.

People with low vision often benefit from line highlighting tools that help them track their position on a page. This guide follows your cursor and highlights the current line of text, reducing visual fatigue and improving reading speed. Many people with nystagmus, macular degeneration, or processing difficulties find such tools invaluable for daily reading tasks. Hover over this text to activate the guide — move your cursor up and down to follow the lines.
